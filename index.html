We are a professional car washing and detailing service located on the Pune-Solapur Highway. Our goal is to provide quality care for your vehicle using the best products and skilled staff.

Welcome to Sai Datta Washing Centre, your trusted partner in vehicle care. Located in Pune, we are dedicated to providing top-quality washing and cleaning services for all types of vehicles — including cars, bikes, rickshaws, tractors, and more.

With a team of experienced staff and modern equipment, we ensure every vehicle is treated with care and leaves our centre looking spotless. From a simple wash to full interior cleaning and polishing, we offer a range of packages to suit your needs and budget.

At Sai Datta Washing Centre, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We believe in honest service, timely delivery, and reasonable pricing. Whether you're a daily commuter or a transport business owner, we’re here to keep your vehicles clean and shining.

Visit us today and experience the best in professional vehicle washing!
